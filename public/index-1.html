About

I am currently a college student working to obtain my Bachelor of Science Degree in Electrical Engineering. My passion is for math and science as well as hardware and software. In my free time my hobbies include working on projects with Arduino, circuits, and programming. I have completed my Associate of Science Degree in Electrical Technology and I am working on my Associate's in Math and Science as prerequisites to transfer to California State University Long Beach extension program in Lancaster.

I enjoy learning programming and building circuits and hardware. I have taught myself various programming languages in my free time and coded some programs for fun. I also enjoy building things for projects and coding them, for example, my LED matrix Arduino project.

I have over six years of experience working in customer service, assisting customers’ questions and concerns. I have worked in the restaurant business at a pizza restaurant and as a technician for a tire company. I am currently working in an Air Force Pathways Student Intern Management Assistant position.

My core values are equivalent to Air Force's three core values: integrity first, service before self, and excellence in all we do. I believe these are great core values for all businesses and industries to suceed. The Core Values are much more than minimum standards. They remind us what it takes to get the mission done. They inspire us to do our very best at all times. They are the common bond among all comrades in arms, and they are the glue that unifies the force and ties us to the great warriors and public servants of the past.
